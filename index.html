Welcome! I am a PhD student in the IDEAL Lab at Northwestern. I am interested in data-driven methods for engineering design, with application in materials.

Before starting graduate school, I earned my BS in Materials Science & Engineering and BEng in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. As an undergraduate researcher I worked in MaGIC at SJTU, where I developed informatics tools for high-throughput phase mapping. I also had a wonderful time working with the Semiconductor Group and Interfaces Lab at Oxford.
